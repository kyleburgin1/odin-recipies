Pizza Recipe

\

A simple pizza recipie that is easy and fast to make.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza base
  • Pizza sauce
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180 celcius (356 farenheight)
  2. Start by putting the sauce on the pizza base
  3. Add the ham and pepperoni
  4. Sprinkle some cheese on top
  5. Throw it in the oven for 10-15 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
